My Music
Application

My Music application, created using Android Studio

This application is an attempt at creating a music app that functions offline with songs that are on the users phone. There are already a few app like this that exist in the play store, and this one I created to allow myself to listen to music and avoid the multitude of ads that come with downloading software form the play store. The application only has one colour scheme at the moment, but this colour scheme does have a dark mode

The applications currently allows the users to listen to any music that is available in their phone's storage. The audio files are sorted via the most recent songs and when the user plays presses on a song it will direct to the music playing screen. On the music playing screen the user can skip to the next song, go to a previous song, shuffle the playlist, repeat a single song multiple times or even just play a single song then stop playing music after that song. The users are also able to compress down the current play session with the button available and they will go back to the music screen with the mini player now filled aloowing them to use it to go to the next or previous song and pause or play the current song.
